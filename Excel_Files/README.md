This project analyzes the sales and financial performance of AtliQ Hardwares using Microsoft Excel dashboards.

## Objective
To analyze customer performance, market trends, and profit & loss from 2019 to 2021 and provide data-driven insights for business decision making.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel

## Dashboards
The Excel dashboards provide:
- Customer-wise net sales and growth analysis
- Market-wise performance vs 2021 targets
- Profit & Loss summary
- KPI and trend visualization

## Business Value
This analysis helps identify:
- High growth customers such as Amazon, Flipkart, Walmart, and AtliQ Exclusive
- Underperforming markets compared to targets
- Overall revenue and profitability trends
